Headline : Rectified power
Date : 18th October ,2016
As we like to drink purified water to keep our body healthy, one can keep their machines healthy by feeding rectified power using Online UPS. It was said by Mr. Momluk Sabir Ahmed, Managing Director, Ark Power Limited on a seminar in Chittagong

A Seminar on “UPS for Sustainable Power System” held in Chittagong on 18th October 2016, Tuesday.

The Seminar Program held in Zinnia Hall, The Peninsula Chittagong and the program organized by Ark Power Limited. Key note presentation delivered by Mr. Roberto Deri, Regional Sales manager, Asia Pacific, Riello UPS.

Some selected Engineers & Key Persons of different Industries in the seminar was invited as the delegate. In the Key note speaking, Mr. Deri introduced some new modern technologies to establish a sustainable Power Qauality and by using those technologies anyone can maintain a sustainable production system. He also introduced some of UPS models of Riello UPS and he illustrated how can those models of Riello UPS can play a significant role to maintain a sustainable power system. He finished his speech with a colorful QA(Question & Answer) Session.

At the end of the session Mr. Monirul Mowla, CEO & Director of Ark Power Limited introduced Ark Power Limited and delivered a presentation on Power Failure Case Study. In his session he took some scenarios of different industries that how they are calculating a huge amount of production losses due to absence of quality power.

Mr. Momluk Sabir Ahmed, Managing Director of Ark Power Limited presented as the chairman of the seminar. Mr. Ahmed said, “We everybody like to drink purified water in the morning and always like to remember that when we drink water. But why we try to feed a Power to our machines without rectifications?”. He said, “You know that different types of noises are present in the national Power Line, which decreases the life time of our machines, and it hampers our production lines, and we have to count the losses year after year. But if anyone use quality qower through Online UPS then he/she can minimize his year after year production losses. And his invested money on UPS which is very much smaller than the Production losses. And in this way we can get back our invested money on UPS almost within one year. The seminar ended through a dinner Session.
